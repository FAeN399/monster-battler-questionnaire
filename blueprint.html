Overview

Player Fantasy: Monster breeding and training and battling
What Makes It Different: Fast paced action packed battles
"Hell Yeah" Moment: Finding a cool combination of breeding, using awesome abilities, and using fun tactics and timing with your monsters
Combat Feel: Digimon World 1, but faster paced
Player Role: Commander and coach
Progression Feel: Mix of grind and journey
Cut First If Needed: Base building and item crafting
2

Monsters

Species

  • 4 species: Insect, Mammal, Reptilian, Avian
  • 4 starter types per species (16 base monsters)

Generations

  • Gen 1: Starters (4 per species)
  • Gen 2: Bred from Gen 1 starters of same species
  • Gen 3: Bred from Gen 2 of same species (25 total types per species)
  • Gen 4: Secret cross-species breeding from certain Gen 3 monsters (6+ unique)
  • Total: 100+ monsters

Party & Storage

  • 2 monsters in active party
  • Storage starts with fence holding 6
  • Upgradeable base storage, maybe 20 max

Acquisition

  • Start with 1 monster
  • Subdue wild monsters
  • Breeding/fusing monsters of same species

Uniqueness

  • Species
  • Generation
  • Birthmarks (random at birth, affect temperament and stat growth)

Relationship

  • Monsters are partners
  • Temperament altered by relationship
  • Treating monsters right increases temperament

Lifespan/Death

  • TBD, maybe injuries and potential death through combat
3

Breeding

Mechanic

  • Either fusion or breeding
  • Long cooldown before monsters can breed again

Eligibility

  • Monster must reach certain age

Inheritance

  • Stats depend on parents using a formula (formula TBD)
  • If parents know moves the child can learn, child keeps them

Breeding Rules

  • Gen 1 + Gen 1 (same species) → Gen 2
  • Gen 2 + Gen 2 (same species only) → Gen 3
  • Certain Gen 3 + Gen 3 (cross-species) → Gen 4

Combat

Pacing

  • 100% Real time

Battle Formats

  • 1v1, 1v2, 2v2
  • Both arena and field battles

Monster Autonomy

  • Depends on Intelligence and Temperament stats
  • Lower INT = can't understand commands
  • Lower Temperament = won't listen due to lack of training

Player Controls (Controller)

Monster Selection

  • Hold L1 = Monster 1
  • Hold R1 = Monster 2
  • L1+L2 = extra options M1
  • R1+R2 = extra options M2

Stances (D-pad)

  • Aggressive
  • Defensive
  • Supportive
  • Passive

Attacks

  • A, B, X, Y buttons
  • 8 attacks per monster
  • Hold L1/R1 to order

Items

  • Hold L2 or R2

Stats

Stat Grows By Affects
STR Attacking HP and Power
INT Using abilities Special Power, listen to orders
AGI Moving around Movement speed, reaction time
END Getting hit HP, Energy Replenishment

(Needs more fleshing out)

World

Structure

  • Island with 4 cities
  • 4 sections, each themed around a species
  • ~20 screens of wilderness per section
  • Caves and other structures to explore
  • Start in Insect section

Monster Capture

  1. Weaken the monster
  2. Tranquilize it
  3. Carry it back to base
  • Can only carry 1 at a time
  • If you die on the way back, you lose it
  • Rarer monsters are harder to return with

Goal

  • Become champion of all arenas
  • Can continue playing afterwards
  • Maybe multiplayer
6

Arena

Structure

  • Tournament brackets determine rank in specific arena
  • Ladder determines overall place
  • Ranks: D, C, B, A, S

Progression

  • Rank B in Arena 1 → move to Arena 2
  • Rank S in Arena 1 → skip to Arena 3
  • Exact values TBD

Rewards

  • Money, Items, Ingredients, Foods
  • Access to new areas

Weight Classes

  • None - can bring godlike Gen 4 to D rank if desired
