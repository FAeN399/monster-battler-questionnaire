3. Monsters

Confirmed

Party Size

2 monsters active at once. Switch control between them using R1/L1.

Acquisition

  • Start with 1 monster
  • Subdue wild monsters (weaken → tranquilize → carry back to base)
  • Breeding/fusing monsters of the same species

Species (4 Total)

Generation System

  • Gen 1: 4 starter types per species (16 total)
  • Gen 2: Gen 1 + Gen 1 breeding (same species)
  • Gen 3: Gen 2 + Gen 2 breeding (same species) → 25 types per species
  • Gen 4: Secret cross-species hybrids (6+ unique monsters)

Total: ~100+ monster variants

What Makes Each Monster Unique

  • Species — Base type and abilities
  • Generation — Power tier and breeding potential
  • Birthmarks — Random traits affecting temperament and stat growth

4. Breeding System

Confirmed

Mechanic

Fusion or breeding with a long cooldown before monsters can breed again.

Eligibility

Monster must reach a certain age to be eligible for breeding/fusing.

Inheritance

  • Stats depend on parents using a formula
  • If parents know moves the child can learn, it keeps them
Open Question: What's the inheritance formula?
Example: (ParentA + ParentB) / 2 * RandomModifier ?

Breeding Rules

  • Gen 1 + Gen 1 (same species) → Gen 2
  • Gen 2 + Gen 2 (same species only) → Gen 3
  • Certain Gen 3 + Gen 3 (cross-species) → Gen 4 (secret)

5. Combat System

Confirmed

Battle Pacing

100% Real-time

Battle Formats

  • 1v1 (Arena & Field)
  • 1v2 (Arena & Field)
  • 2v2 (Arena & Field)

Monster Autonomy

Monsters control themselves based on:
  • Intelligence: Lower INT = poor command comprehension
  • Temperament: Lower TEMP = ignores commands (lack of training)
Treating monsters right increases temperament.

Player Commands

InputAction
D-PadStance: Aggressive / Defensive / Supportive / Passive
L1 / R1Switch control between Monster 1 / Monster 2
L1 + A/B/X/YOrder Monster 1 to use Attack 1-4
R1 + A/B/X/YOrder Monster 2 to use Attack 1-4
L1+L2 + A/B/X/YMonster 1 Attacks 5-8 (or combo)
R1+R2 + A/B/X/YMonster 2 Attacks 5-8 (or combo)
L2 / R2Use items
Open Question: How does this translate to Keyboard/Mouse?

Tactics

  • Pincer attack
  • Attack separate monsters
  • Attack same monster
  • Special combo attack (L1+L2 / R1+R2)

6. World & Exploration

Confirmed

Setting

Island with 4 regions, each themed around a species.

Structure

  • 4 cities (one per region)
  • ~20 screens of wilderness per region
  • Enterable areas: caves, structures
  • Progress by ranking up in arena to unlock next region
Open Question: Seamless open world or distinct map loads between screens?

Wilderness Content

  • Wild monsters to fight and capture
  • Items: heals, power-ups
  • Food ingredients for cooking
  • Caves/structures with rare items and boss monsters

Wild Monster Capture

  1. Weaken the monster
  2. Tranquilize it
  3. Carry it back to base (can only carry 1)
  4. If you die on the way back, you lose it
Rarer monsters = harder to return with.

Day/Night & Seasons

TBD — If any, probably won't affect spawns or behaviors.

7. Arena System

Confirmed

Structure

  • Tournament brackets determine rank within an arena
  • Ladder determines overall placement
  • Ranks: D → C → B → A → S

Progression

Achieving certain rank unlocks path to next area.
Example: Rank B in Arena 1 → Access to Arena 2. Rank S in Arena 1 → Skip to Arena 3.
Exact rank requirements TBD.

Rewards

  • Money
  • Items and ingredients
  • Foods
  • Access to new areas

Weight Classes

None. Bring your godlike Gen 4 to D-rank if you want.

Goal

Become champion of all arenas. Game continues after completion.
