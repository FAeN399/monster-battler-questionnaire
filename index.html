Core Features

🧬

Deep Breeding System

4 species with 4 generations each. Breed monsters of the same species to unlock new forms. Certain Gen 3 combos unlock secret Gen 4 cross-species hybrids.

⚡

Fast-Paced Combat

100% real-time battles inspired by Digimon World. Command your monsters with tactical stances, direct attacks, and precisely-timed abilities.

🏆

Arena Progression

Rise through D to S rank across 4 themed arenas. Tournament brackets determine your rank. Become the grand champion of them all.
